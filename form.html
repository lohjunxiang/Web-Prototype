Our services rely on your donations.

We rely on your donated items to support The Salvation Army programs.


Please make sure the goods you donate are of good quality. The Salvation Army spends million of dollars each year to remove rubbish at has been dumped at our Donation Bins

What we can accept:

Clothings and Accessories

Clothing, footwear and accessories in good condition.

Bric-A-Brac and Homewares

Household items that are in good condition.

Toys, Books, CDs, DVDs and Vinyl

Items that are in good condition.

Electrical Goods

Small electrical items such as radios in good, working, condition.

White Goods

Items such as refrigerators in good working condition.

Quality Furniture

Furniture that is not broken, stained or torn.

If you're unsure whether your items are donable, please use this form to enquire about donable items. Or call us at 13 SALVOS (13 72 58).
